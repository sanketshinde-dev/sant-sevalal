The Vision of Sant Sevalal

Who We Are

Sant Sevalal Banjara Pratishthan is a registered NGO dedicated to the holistic development of the Gor-Banjara community. We are inspired by the revolutionary teachings of Sant Sevalal Maharaj.

Our primary focus is to eradicate social evils like addiction and dowry, while promoting education, women's empowerment, and cultural heritage across Maharashtra.
